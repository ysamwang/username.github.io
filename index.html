About Me

CV

I am currently a post-doc in the Booth School at the University of Chicago working with Mladen Kolar. Previously, I completed my PhD in Statistics at the University of Washington under the supervision of Mathias Drton. My research interests include graphical models, causal discovery, and high dimensional statistics.

I was an undergraduate at Rice University, and outside of academia I have worked in management consulting, finance, and environmental regulation.

In my free time, I enjoy playing soccer, attempting to cook, riding my bike and stressing about the Dallas Cowboys.
